Slow Poison  
(Necromancy)  
  
Level: 2  
Sphere: Healing   
Range: Touch   
Duration: Instant  
Casting Time: 1   
Area of Effect: 1 creature   
Saving Throw: None   
  
When this spell is placed upon a poisoned individual, it greatly slows the effects of venom, actually neutralizing all but the most deadly poisons.
