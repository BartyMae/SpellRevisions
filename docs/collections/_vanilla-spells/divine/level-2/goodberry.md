Goodberry  
(Alteration, Evocation)  
  
Level: 2  
Sphere: Plant   
Range: Touch   
Duration: Permanent  
Casting Time: 9   
Area of Effect: Special   
Saving Throw: None   
  
Casting a Goodberry spell creates 5 magical berries that the caster can carry with &lt;PRO_HIMHER&gt;. Each berry restores 1 Hit Point when eaten.
