Shillelagh  
(Alteration)  
  
Level: 1  
Sphere: Combat, Plant   
Range: 0  
Duration: 4 rounds + 1 round/level   
Casting Time: 2   
Area of Effect: Special  
Saving Throw: None   
  
This spell enables the caster to create a magical cudgel that has a +1 bonus to its attack roll and inflicts 2d4 points of damage on opponents.
