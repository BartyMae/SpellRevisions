Horror  
(Necromancy)  
  
Level: 2  
Range: 40 ft.  
Duration: 1 turn  
Casting Time: 2  
Area of Effect: 15-ft. radius  
Saving Throw: Neg.  
  
All enemies within the area of effect when this spell is cast must save vs. Spell or flee in terror. Certain creatures are immune to the effects of fear, including all undead.
