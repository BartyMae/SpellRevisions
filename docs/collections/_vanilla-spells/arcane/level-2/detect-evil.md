Detect Evil  
(Divination)  
  
Level: 2  
Range: 0  
Duration: Instant  
Casting Time: 2  
Area of Effect: 30-ft. radius  
Saving Throw: None  
  
This spell discovers emanations of evil from any creature. Any evil creature within the range of the spell will glow red briefly.
