Banishment  
Level: 6  
School: Abjuration  
Sphere: Wards  
Range: Long  
Duration: Instant  
Casting Time: 6  
Area of Effect: 30' radius  
Saving Throw: None  
  
This spell forces hostile summoned creatures in the area back to their proper plane. Magic resistance does not effect this spell.
