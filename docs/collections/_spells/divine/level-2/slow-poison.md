Slow Poison  
Level: 2  
School: Necromancy  
Sphere: Healing  
Range: Touch  
Duration: Instant  
Casting Time: 1  
Area of Effect: 1 creature  
Saving Throw: None  
  
When this spell is cast upon a poisoned individual, it greatly slows the effects of venom, actually neutralizing all but the most deadly poisons.
