Pixie Dust  
Level: 5  
School: Conjuration  
Sphere: Combat  
Range: Personal  
Duration: 1 turn  
Casting Time: 5  
Area of Effect: 30' radius  
Saving Throw: None  
  
This spell creates a handful of pixie dust that the caster can toss into the air. Allies covered by the dust becomes improved invisible for 1 turn.
