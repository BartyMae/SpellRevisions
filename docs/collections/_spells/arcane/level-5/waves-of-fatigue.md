Waves of Fatigue  
Level: 5  
School: Necromancy  
Range: Long  
Duration: 5 rounds  
Casting Time: 5  
Area of Effect: 30' radius  
Saving Throw: None  
  
Surges of negative energy render all hostile living creatures in the spell's area fatigued, as if they haven't rested in a day. Multiple castings of this spell are cumulative.
